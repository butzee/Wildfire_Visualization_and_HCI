Motivation

As of the development of this application (June 2023), the city of New York is coated in thick red smoke.

The reason are huge wildfires, which in the last few years have almost become a normal part in the life of millions of americans.

Wildfires can become very dangerous for both nature and humans, especially since the rapid increase of temperatures, caused by climate change, has led to a big increase in wildfires.

Therefore, it is important to understand the causes and extend of wildfires, in order to raise awareness and prevent them from happening in the future.

This project shines light on the question: Is america in flames?

About the application

This application visualizes wildfires in the US from 1992 till 2020. It enables the rendering of either cumulated wildfires for each year, or even wildfires for each day of a selected year.

It features the options to filter the rendered wildfires by a large set of causes, including e.g. arson, as well as burned acres.

The application also allows the user to set the playback speed and the scaling of the rendered wildires, as well as the option to display the wildfires as points or clusters.

Depending on the user preference, different kinds of map styles can be displayed dynamically, even during the animation.
